# Prompt for Audience Persona

Create an audience positioning analysis for ecommerce image generation.

Define:

- primary buyer and shopping moment
- secondary buyers if relevant
- buying motivations
- purchase objections
- aesthetic preferences
- platform behavior hypotheses
- scene priority
- copy voice and words to avoid
- conversion intent: click reason, trust reason, comparison reason

Tie assumptions to product category, platform/category research, visible product traits, and provided user context. Label uncertainty clearly. Do not invent demographic certainty.

Return structured outputs. Do not exceed the Skill boundary.
